Zodiac Signs

There are 12 zodiac signs, and they are determined based on people's date of birth. Each sign has its own strengths/weaknesses, specific traits, desires, and attitude of life. The astrology behind each sign can give a glimpse of a person's basic characteristics, preferences, flaws, fears, and your relationships.



The signs in order are:
  1. Aries
  2. Taurus
  3. Gemini
  4. Cancer
  5. Leo
  6. Virgo
  7. Libra
  8. Scorpio
  9. Sagittarius
  10. Capricorn
  11. Aquarius
  12. Pisces
