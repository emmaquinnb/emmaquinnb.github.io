Stout

Characteristics

  • Strong roasted malt taste
  • Hints of coffee, chocolate, and carmel
  • Dark body almost black
  • Features a rich and creamy head
    •

    Food Pairings

  • Roasted/smoked foods
  • Barbecued/Grilled foods
  • Salty foods
  • Oysters
  • Rich stews
  • Braised dishes
  • Desserts (ideally the beer is sweeter than the dish)
    •

    About

    Stouts are similar to porters but are stronger and have a deep roast flavor. They are dark in color and has more hop added than porters.
